## 一、核心定位变化

### 旧定位
> "Fully local, never uploaded. Zero cloud."

### 新定位
> "Your data, your choice.
> Local by default. Hybrid when you need it. Enterprise on-premise when you must.
> Your audit ledger never trains anyone else's model. Ever."

**三句话解释差异：**
- 旧定位是一个技术约束，把自己锁死
- 新定位是一个用户权利，把三类用户都装进来
- "数据不训练别人的模型"才是用户真正在意的那件事

### 需要新写的内容

**1. README.md 新增「Deployment Modes」章节**
```
## Deployment Modes

K9 Audit is local by default. Your audit ledger lives on your machine.
No data leaves unless you configure it to.

| Mode | Who it's for | Data location |
|------|-------------|---------------|
| Local (default) | Individual devs, sensitive projects | Your disk only |
| Optional sync (Phase 2) | Teams wanting shared dashboards | Encrypted, you control the key |
| Self-hosted (Phase 2) | Compliance-driven orgs | Your infrastructure |
| Enterprise on-premise | Financial, medical, government | Air-gapped if needed |

The audit ledger is yours. K9 will never train on your data.
```

**2. README.md FAQ 新条目**
```
**Will K9 ever train on my audit data?**
No. K9 Audit produces evidence ledgers that belong to you.
We do not have access to your local ledger.
If you opt into future sync features, data is encrypted before leaving your machine
and we will never use it to train models.
```
