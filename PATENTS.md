# Patent Notice

## Covered Patents

The methods and architecture implemented in K9log are covered by the following patent application(s):

- **U.S. Provisional Patent Application No. 63/981,777**
  "Causal Intervention-Effect Unit (CIEU): A Universal Causal Record Architecture for Audit and Governance of Arbitrary Processes"
  Filed: 2025

## Scope

The patent covers, among other things:

- The CIEU 5-tuple record structure (X_t, U_t, Y*_t, Y_{t+1}, R_{t+1})
- SHA256 hash chain integrity for audit records
- L1 causal DAG construction and root cause identification
- L2 counterfactual replay with alternative policies
- Y*_t Intent Contract versioning and hashing
- Profile-O (Observer) and Profile-G (Governor) operational modes
